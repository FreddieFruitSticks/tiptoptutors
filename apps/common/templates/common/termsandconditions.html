Where we came from
Our story in a nutshell.


Hi, my name is Freddie and I founded Tip Top Tutors while doing my Master’s degree in Mathematical Statistics at the University of Cape Town. I found that there is an increasing need for tutoring in mathematical-based subjects. We are specialised in these subjects and, as such, our tutors know exactly how to guide you, and how to help you achieve your goals.

We believe that a tutoring agency should be more than just a ‘middle-man’. That is why at Tip Top Tutors we guarantee results and therefore I, personally, hand-pick only the very best and brightest tutors. Each tutor has a complete background check before they are allowed to tutor, and each pupil tutored by Tip Top Tutors is closely monitored to ensure that their needs are met.

Why we guarantee results
Our policy on quality education.


If you purchase a product from a store, every now and then you will get a 'bad apple'. The only thing to do is return this product. Unfortunately, we cannot have the private lessons that were tutored returned (since you cannot return the tutor’s time like you can a tangible product), but we can offer to tutor the pupil for free to ensure their marks do actually improve. That is why we are prepared to offer the pupil an extra 3 months of tutoring absolutely free if his/her marks do not increase when using our tutors. This is our quality assurance guarantee.

Why our tutors work best
Screened, qualified and specialised.


Private tuition is a method of education that has, in our experience, proven to improve grades better than any other method of extra-curricular activity. It gives pupils the opportunity to discuss their particular difficulties, allows the tutor to teach in the exact way that allows each different pupil to learn best, and the pupil is compelled to engage with the tutor without any distractions.

Our systems of tuition are all geared towards mathematical-based subjects and our tutors are all mathematically-inclined, and numerically-talented, individuals. We monitor each pupil on a weekly basis and encourage tutors to take a mentor-like approach when tutoring the pupil. This way our pupils want to learn, which is a stack-load better than forced learning (trust us!).
